Privacy Policy for OPPL Mobile

Last updated: April 2026

OPPL Mobile (and the OPPL web app) is operated by Kelsey Dougherty (“we,” “us,” or “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

  • Account Data: email address, first and last name, user role (admin, representative, or user), and membership-related information.
  • League Data: team assignments, lineup selections, match and scorecard entries, and timestamps.
  • Technical Data: device type, operating system, and basic logs used for diagnostics, performance, and security.

How We Use Information

  • Authenticate users and manage access to app features.
  • Operate league management, scheduling, and scoring functionality.
  • Maintain and improve app performance and reliability.
  • Provide support and respond to user inquiries.
  • Ensure security and prevent misuse.

Data Storage and Processing

We use Supabase, a third-party service provider, to store and manage application data, including authentication and database services. Your data may be securely processed and stored on Supabase infrastructure.

Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data.

We may share information only:

  • With service providers (such as Supabase) strictly to operate the app
  • If required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of users or the application

Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active and as needed to support league operations and historical records. You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data; however, certain league records may be retained where necessary for legitimate purposes.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) and controlled access to stored data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

OPPL Mobile is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the minimum legal age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights and Choices

You may request access to your data, correction of inaccurate information, or deletion of your account and personal data by contacting us. You may stop using the app at any time; uninstalling the app does not automatically delete your stored data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted with a revised “Last updated” date.
