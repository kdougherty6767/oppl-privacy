Privacy Policy for OPPL Mobile

Last updated: Nov 28 2025

OPPL Mobile (and the OPPL web app) is operated by Kelsey Dougherty (“we,” “us”). We use Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore to provide league management, lineup, and scoring features.

Information We Collect

  • Account data: email address, first/last name, role (admin/rep/user), membership expiry, handicap.
  • League data: team assignments, lineup selections, match/scorecard entries, timestamps.
  • Technical data: device/OS information and basic logs for diagnostics and security.

How We Use Information

  • Authenticate users and authorize features.
  • Manage teams, schedules, lineups, and scoring.
  • Communicate service-related updates and support.
  • Maintain security and prevent misuse.

Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent personal data. We may share with service providers (e.g., Firebase) only to operate the app. We may disclose if required by law or to protect rights and security.

Data Retention

We keep data while your account is active and while league records are needed. You may request deletion of your account data; some league records may be retained for legitimate purposes.

Security

Data is transmitted over TLS and stored in Firebase with access controls. No method is 100% secure.

Children’s Privacy

Not intended for children under 13 (or under the minimum age in your region). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your Choices

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting us. You may stop using the app at any time; uninstalling does not automatically delete stored account data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be posted with an updated “Last updated” date.
